Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Claude AI Flashcard Generator ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our application and website. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • User-Provided Information: Documents you upload for flashcard generation, API keys you provide, and any customizations you make to flashcards.
  • Usage Information: Information about how you use the application, including learning progress, correct/incorrect answers, and study patterns.
  • Technical Information: Information about your device and connection, including browser type, IP address, and operating system.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Process and generate flashcards from your uploaded documents
  • Track and display your learning progress
  • Securely connect with third-party APIs using your provided credentials
  • Personalize educational rewards based on your learning patterns
  • Troubleshoot and debug technical issues

4. Data Storage and Processing

Our application primarily processes data locally in your browser, with the following exceptions:

  • API calls to Claude AI for processing documents (if enabled)
  • API calls to TikTok for educational reward videos (if enabled)

API keys are securely transmitted when making API calls but are not permanently stored on our servers.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party APIs used to deliver functionality (Anthropic's Claude API, TikTok API)
  • Legal Compliance: When required by applicable law, legal process, or government requests

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing and receiving a copy of your data
  • Correcting or deleting your personal information
  • Objecting to our processing of your information
  • Withdrawing consent where applicable

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details in Section 10.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no electronic transmission or storage technology is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date at the top.

10. Contact Us
